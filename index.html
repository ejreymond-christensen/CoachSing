Coach Tuulikki

Tuulikki has studied classical singing and theory and history of classical music in Kuopio High School of Music and Dance, Kuopio Conservatory and Mikkeli Music School in Finland completing an advanced exam Classical Singing in 2005. She has sang in different choirs for more than 30 years and has a diverse drama and amateur theatre experience from Finnish summer theatres as well as from Rogaland International theatre BATS. In 2005 she sang in the production of Aida in the choir of Savonlinna Opera Festival Choir in Finland and in 2013 in the choir of Norwegian National Opera and Stavanger Symphony Orcestra production of La Traviata.

In BATS Tuulikki has acted and also been the musical director and vocal coach in several productions (The Majestic 2017, The Frog Prince 2017, Little Red Riding Hood 2014, Sleeping Beauty 2012, 1001 Arabian nights 2010) She also has a Neuro-Linguistic Programming - practitioner certificate and has worked as a Continuous Improvement Specialist working with business coaching and problem solving coaching.
